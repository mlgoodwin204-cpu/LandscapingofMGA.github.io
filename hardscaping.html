Hardscaping:

Hardscaping adds an element of flare to any house or landscaping project, while also protecting it. Gravel, brick, rock, decks, patios, and retaining walls are just some of the features hardscaping offers. They are functional yet stylish and work to keep your yards safe from soil erosion, flooding, weed control, and dirt runoff.

After a consulation and depending on your yard's layout, we can help you decide the best course of action to take. Gravel, mulch, and rocks work best for garden beds to prevent weed infestations. Retaining walls work best with sloped yards to prevent water and dirt runoff from settling in the yard and messing it up. Bricks and rocks also make great pathway guides for sidewalks and driveways.
